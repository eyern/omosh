A little about Omosh Interiors

We are a registered and one of the best interior design companies in Kenya. Specializing in providing stunning and excellent interior design products services that include:- space planning, designing and project management for both residential and elegant commercial projects in Kenya and East Africa region.

Here at Omosh Interiors believe that every client has unique requirements and taste. And in order to support that, Omosh Interiors comprises of a team which can understand your ideas and add up to the best and most creative designs in order to make your dreams become a reality.

We have endless ideas in mind that can impress you at every step. We also strive for the satisfaction of each and every client, from conceptualization to the last stage.
